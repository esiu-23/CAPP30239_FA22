During the 102nd session of the Illinois Congress, 1,838 bills were introduced. Of those, 1,102 became law.

The most common action taken during the 102nd Illinois congress was referring bills to committee. One bill can be referred to committee multiple times. In total, 35,916 referrals occurred, which averages to about 19 referrals per bill. The next most common actions are readings, where the bill is read on the floor. There are first, second, and third readings. The most common reading by far is the first reading, which occurred 12,500 times. Second and third readings only occurred a combined 10,144 times.
